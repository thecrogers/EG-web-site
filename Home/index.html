Why We're Here

We love our tight-knit church.

"Becoming memebers of Elm Grove was definitely a God thing for us. We were looking for a church to call home and found a flyer for Elm Grove on our gate. We decided to give it a try and haven't had a second thought about where we want to be since. We immediately were welcomed into the church and Brother Bill's straight-forward Bible-based teaching has changed our lives. We are so thankful that Elm Grove found us and that we are now a part of it's loving tight-knit church."

- Marianne Wienecke
